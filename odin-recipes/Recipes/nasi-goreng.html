Nasi Goreng

Description

This is recipe to make Indonesian autentic Nasi Goreng

Ingredients

  • 1 portion of Cold Rice
  • 2 Garlic
  • 2 Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 3 Bird eye chili
  • 2 Tablespoon of Oil
  • 1 Tablespoon of Soy Sauce
  • MSG

Steps

  1. Heat up oil until smokey in wok
  2. Chop garlic and bird eye chili then add to hot oil
  3. After you can smell the garlic fragrant, add 2 eggs and stir
  4. Add cold rice to wok
  5. Put seasoning such salt, pepper, and don't forget MSG
  6. Your nasi goreng ready to serve
