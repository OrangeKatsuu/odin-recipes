Indomie Goreng

Description

This is recipe to cook indomie , Indonesian GOATED instant noodle

Ingredients

  • 1 Indomie Instant Noodle
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Green Onion
  • Water

Steps

  1. Boil two glasses water in a pan
  2. Then, open the package of Indomie Instant noodles and put to the pan with egg
  3. While waiting for the water to boil, pour the seasoning: chili sauce, soya sauce and oil into a bowl
  4. After the water is boiled, drain the noodles from water
  5. Pour the noodle into bowl
  6. After that, mix noodles with the seasoning
  7. Now your noodle is ready
